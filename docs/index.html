What it does
🎵

Browse your library

See every song on your iPod with real track names, artists, and albums. Search and sort instantly.

📂

Add any audio file

Drag and drop MP3, FLAC, WAV, OGG, WMA, AIFF, or OPUS files. Non-native formats are auto-converted.

▶

Download from YouTube

Paste any YouTube link and the song is downloaded, converted to MP3, and added to your iPod in one click.

🔊

Play songs in the browser

Stream any track from your iPod directly in the app with a full playback bar — play, pause, skip, scrub.

⚙

Auto format conversion

FFmpeg converts anything to AAC automatically. You never need to think about whether a file will work.

🔒

Fully local & private

Runs entirely on your Mac. No accounts, no cloud, no data sent anywhere. Your music stays yours.

How it works
1

Download the app

Download the Mac app below and move it to your Applications folder.

2

Plug in your iPod

Connect your iPod via USB. Enable "Manually manage music" in Finder if prompted.

3

Double-click to open

The app opens your browser automatically at localhost:5050. Click Detect iPod.

4

Add your music

Drag files in, paste YouTube links, or browse what's already on your iPod.
